Description

Beer cheese soup is a rich and creamy soup that combines the flavors of sharp cheddar cheese and beer for a comforting and indulgent dish. The soup starts with a base of sautéed onions and garlic, which are then simmered in a mixture of beer and chicken broth. Sharp cheddar cheese is melted into the soup, creating a velvety texture and a bold flavor. The soup is often finished with a touch of cream for added richness. Beer cheese soup is perfect for chilly days and is often served with crusty bread or soft pretzels for dipping.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup beer (lager or ale works well)
  • 2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped chives or green onions for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large pot, melt butter over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, sauté until translucent.
  2. Stir in flour and cook for 1-2 minutes to form a roux.
  3. Gradually whisk in chicken broth and beer, ensuring no lumps form. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Reduce heat and stir in shredded cheddar cheese until melted and smooth.
  5. Stir in heavy cream and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the soup thickens.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with chopped chives or green onions if desired.
