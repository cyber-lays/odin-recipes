Description

Classic lasagna is built by layering tender lasagna noodles with a rich, savory meat sauce and creamy cheese filling: sautéed onions and garlic are browned with ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork), then simmered with crushed tomatoes, a splash of red wine, and Italian herbs until thick and fragrant; meanwhile, combine ricotta (or béchamel), an egg, and grated Parmesan, and shred plenty of mozzarella. Spread a thin layer of sauce in a baking dish, add noodles, dollops of the ricotta mixture, more sauce, and a generous sprinkling of mozzarella and Parmesan, then repeat layers until the dish is full. Bake at about 375°F (190°C) for 35–45 minutes until the top is golden and bubbling, let it rest 10–15 minutes to set, then slice and serve for a hearty, comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, sauté onion and garlic until translucent. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, red wine, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Layer with noodles, ricotta mixture, more sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake for 35-45 minutes until bubbly and golden on top.
  9. Let rest for 10-15 minutes before serving.
