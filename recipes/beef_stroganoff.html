Description

Beef Stroganoff is a classic Russian dish that features tender strips of beef cooked in a creamy mushroom sauce, typically served over egg noodles or rice. The dish is known for its rich flavors and comforting texture. To prepare Beef Stroganoff, start by sautéing onions and mushrooms in a skillet until they are soft and fragrant. Then, add thinly sliced beef (such as sirloin or tenderloin) and cook until browned. Next, create the sauce by adding beef broth, sour cream, and a touch of Dijon mustard to the skillet, allowing it to simmer until thickened. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika for added depth of flavor. Finally, serve the beef stroganoff over cooked egg noodles or rice, garnished with fresh parsley for a delicious and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef sirloin or tenderloin, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Paprika (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Egg noodles or rice for serving

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, sauté until translucent.
  2. Add sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and become tender.
  3. Add the thinly sliced beef to the skillet and cook until browned on all sides. Remove beef and set aside.
  4. Sprinkle flour over the onion and mushroom mixture, stirring to combine and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Gradually pour in beef broth, stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Bring to a simmer.
  6. Stir in sour cream and Dijon mustard, mixing well to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Return the cooked beef to the skillet, stirring to coat with the sauce. Simmer for a few minutes until heated through.
  8. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika to taste.
  9. Serve the beef stroganoff over cooked egg noodles or rice, garnished with fresh parsley.
